About Me

I am Evan Hardek and right now I am a student in The Ohio State University Coding Boot Camp. My first experience with coding was in high school. But earning this certificate is my first step in formalizing the skills I need to enter the industry as a web developer.

I graduated from high school in 2016. I’ve taken a few classes from a local community college. But, for the most part, I have worked full time in a warehouse. It’s your basic warehouse job filling, packing and shipping customer orders.

But -- what’s not so basic -- are the things that I’ve learned about myself and the business world. I’ve figured out the ins and outs of working with a wide range of people with different backgrounds and motivations. I’ve honed my skills for attention to detail and production of quality work. And, I’ve learned that I want more than a good job. I want a meaningful career and know I can find that as a web developer.

When I’m not at work, I freely admit to being a nerd who's always looking for new music or horror movie to watch. I’m most at peace when I’m hiking, spending time near water, or hanging with friends, family and my cat Sylvester.
